my trip to austria

Like the rest of my trips, my trip to Austria was an interesting experience. I visited several locations in Austria including the small but lovely Salzburg and the populous capital city Vienna.

Salzburg

I did not expect to enjoy visitng Salzburg to the degree I did because of how small and secluded it was since I am more of a city person. However, I was wrong for I found comfort in this small city. The nature there was extraordinary and the people were very friendly and the only thing I regret about visiting Salzburg was that I did not stay there longer.

Vienna

During my visit to Vienna, I had visited the massive Schonbrunn Palace which had been a summer home for the Hasburg rulers in the past. The view after climbing to the top of the hill was mesmerizing and the garden was vast and colorful. It was one of the most beautiful places I had ever been to and I wish to go there a second time.
