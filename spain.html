my trip to spain

My trip to Spain was a very exciting trip. I had visited two major cities in Spain which were Madrid and Barcelona. Barcelona stood out to me in particular for its unique architecture specifically the work of Antoni Gaudí. I had visited the Sagrada Familia which was a hauntingly beautiful basilica with an extremely detailed design. What I liked the most about the Sagrada Familia was the contrast between the inside and the outside of the building as it was colorful on the inside and dark on the outside. Though the basilica was unfinished, witnessing it in real life was an experience I could never forget.
